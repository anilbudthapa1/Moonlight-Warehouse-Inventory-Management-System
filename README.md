## 📖 Project Overview
The **Moonlight Warehouse Management System** is a full-stack web application designed for an **Australian e-commerce grocery business**. The purpose of this project is to improve warehouse and inventory operations by replacing manual and fragmented processes with a centralized digital system.

The project addresses common warehouse challenges such as inaccurate stock records, poor inventory visibility, manual stock movement tracking, expiry management issues, and delays in order preparation and dispatch. The proposed system helps improve warehouse efficiency, reduce operational errors, and support better business decision-making.

This system is being developed using:

- **Frontend:** React.js
- **Backend:** Node.js with Express.js
- **Database:** PostgreSQL

The development process follows software engineering practices including **product thinking, market research, system design, agile delivery, testing, deployment, and documentation**.

---

## 🎯 Purpose of the Project
The purpose of this project is to design and develop a web-based warehouse management solution that improves warehouse inventory operations for an Australian e-commerce grocery business. The system aims to reduce manual work, improve stock accuracy, support expiry and batch tracking, manage stock movement efficiently, and provide a centralized platform for warehouse-related activities.

---

## ✅ Objective of the Project
The objective of the Moonlight Warehouse Management System project is to develop a secure, scalable, and user-friendly warehouse inventory management system. The system is intended to streamline stock management, improve inventory visibility, reduce manual errors, support batch and expiry tracking, enhance warehouse efficiency, and generate useful reports for operational decision-making.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement
Moonlight, as an e-commerce grocery business, requires efficient warehouse operations to manage stock, fulfil orders, and reduce wastage. However, manual and disconnected processes can lead to inaccurate stock records, delays, poor stock visibility, and difficulty tracking expiry-sensitive products.

This problem affects warehouse staff, managers, and business stakeholders, and can ultimately reduce customer satisfaction. In the Australian market, where efficiency, reliability, and digital capability are critical, there is a strong need for a modern warehouse management solution.

---

## 📌 Project Scope
The scope of this project includes the design and development of a web-based warehouse inventory system that supports:

- User authentication and role-based access
- Product and category management
- Supplier management
- Inventory tracking
- Stock in and stock out operations
- Batch and expiry monitoring
- Warehouse location management
- Low-stock alerts
- Reporting and dashboard features
- Project documentation and deployment planning

---

## 🚫 Out of Scope
The following items are outside the scope of this project:

- Customer-facing e-commerce shopping website
- Online payment integration
- Delivery route optimization
- Mobile application development
- AI-based demand forecasting
- Accounting and payroll functions
- IoT sensors and warehouse robotics
- Full ERP integration
- International multi-warehouse logistics

---

## 🧩 High-Level Requirements
The Moonlight Warehouse Management System must:

- Provide secure login and role-based access
- Maintain accurate inventory records
- Support stock movement and adjustment tracking
- Track batch numbers and expiry dates
- Manage products, categories, suppliers, and warehouse locations
- Generate reports and dashboard summaries
- Be user-friendly, secure, scalable, and maintainable
- Support future deployment and system enhancement
